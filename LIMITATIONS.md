# LIMITATIONS

Atlas JV Planning OS is a simplified educational simulation and has important limitations.

## General Limitations

This project is not a production-grade planning platform and should not be used for real operational, commercial, financial, engineering, legal, or regulatory decisions.

## Modeling Limitations

### 1. Synthetic data only

All data is ficticious and generated for demonstration purposes. Results do not represent any real asset or organization.

### 2. Simplified economics

Economic calclations are intentionally simplified for transparency and educational value. They may omit or simplify:

- depreciation

- working capital

- financing structure

- detailed tax treatment

- complex royalty mechanisms

- inflation/escalation detail

- abandoment liabilities

- contractual edge cases

### 3. Simplified operations logic

Production, cost, and capital assumptions are modeled at a high level and do not capture the complexity of actual field behavior, facility constraints, subsurface uncertainty, or operating interruptions in full detail.

### 4. Simplified forecasting

Mid-year forecasting logic is designed to illustrate planning workflow, not to serve as a full forecasting engine.

### 5. Simplifid KPI framework

KPIs are intended as educational examples and may not match the exact definitions, formulas, timing logic, or governance standards used by any real company.

### 6. Simplified governance and version control

Any workflow, review, approval, and version tracking features are generic simulations, not reproductions of any real corporate governance system.

### User Interpretation Limitation

Users should interpret outputs as:

- illustrative

- educational

- portfolio-grade demonstrations

Users should not interpret outputs as:

- asset valuations

- certified reserves views

- management-approved plan

- accounting statements

- auditable operational reports

## Future Extension Limitation

Additional modules may be added over time, but the existece of such modules does not remove the simplified and synthetic nature of the application.

## Bottom Line

Atlas JV Planning OS demonstrates structured thinking, analytics, and workflow design. It does not claim to be a substitute for real enterprise systems, engineering tools, finance systems, or internal planning processes.
